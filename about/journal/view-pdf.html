About the Journal

Mission Statement

The Michigan Undergraduate Law Review was founded at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor in 2023 with the vision of facilitating legal scholarship among undergraduates at the university. As a team of editors and writers, our staffers work towards the goal of an annual publication of a professional level law review, formed of external long-form submissions from scholars in academia, as well as notes from our own team.

In addition to fostering intellectual exchange internally, members of the Michigan Undergraduate Law Review have the opportunity to network with and learn from renowned law professors and Juris Doctorate candidates from the University of Michigan Law School.

Scope

The Michigan Undergraduate Law Review publishes content pertaining to the law and other closely related disciplines.
