About the Journal

Mission Statement

The Michigan Undergraduate Law Review is the first and only undergraduate legal publication at the University of Michigan. We provide students interested in careers in law, academia, and policy the opportunity to publish long-form articles on topics in legal scholarship.

As a team of editors and writers, our staffers work towards the goal of producing an annual law review publication, formed of external long-form submissions from scholars in academia, as well as notes from our own team.

Members of the Michigan Undergraduate Law Review also have the opportunity to network with and learn from renowned law professors and Juris Doctorate candidates from the University of Michigan Law School.

Scope

The Michigan Undergraduate Law Review publishes content pertaining to the law and other closely related disciplines.
